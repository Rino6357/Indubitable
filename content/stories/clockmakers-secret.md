Old Elias lived in a shop filled with the rhythmic ticking of a thousand hearts. Every clock in his workshop had its own voice, its own story. But there was one clock, hidden behind a velvet curtain, that never made a sound.

It was a clock that didn't measure time in seconds or minutes. It measured memories.

One day, a young girl named Clara entered the shop. She didn't want a watch or a grandfather clock. She wanted to find a lost moment. Elias smiled, his eyes twinkling like polished brass. He led her to the velvet curtain...
