---
title: "Infection"
date: "2026-04-13"
category: "poem"
---

Dove the blade of utter bleakness,
To martyr the heart of a jade eternal.
A monster scanning for a transient weakness,
A corruption spread of the cunning infernal.

An evil dug into the emerald sacred,
Shattering what could not be broken,
By an envoy of THE KING’s ancient hatred,
Born from a curse he himself had spoken.

Encroaching on the walls of isolation,
A monster sent to destroy a hopeful prophecy.
“I am here to bring upon you an eternal damnation”,
“I am the sword of THE KING’s inevitable sovereignty.”
