---
title: "They asked me"
date: "2025-11-15"
category: "poem"
---

They asked me about the falling leaves, and about the flowing petals.
They asked me about the breathing fog, and about the perching birds.
They asked me about the scent of rain, and about the rainbow mirages.
So I told them of the hulking planets, the smoldering flames, and of the ambitious falcons.
So I told them of the envious cosmos, the rusted engines, and the undug diamonds...
