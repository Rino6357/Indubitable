---
title: "The storm"
date: "2025-11-23"
category: "poem"
---

The storm whispers ancient mystery,
Vibrations ripple through air and space.
Words of a forgotten tongue lost to history, 
Understood only through prayer and grace.

Winds blow violently at the fortress,
Stones crumbling under the unseen.
Visions of death form under the duress, 
Yet the flame soars, existing between.

Wings to either side, lifting infinity,
Thunder hurled into the inferno in vain.
A burning passion, flying with affinity,
A roaring tempest, fire against the grain.

Ambition lifts and rights the disparity,
Feathers shuddering through the pelting.
Logic shifts what's left in the clarity,
The gales blow, swirl, and die melting.
