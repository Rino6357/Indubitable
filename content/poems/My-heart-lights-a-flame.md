---
title: "My heart lights a flame"
date: "2025-05-04"
category: "poem"
---

A bow drawn, a bow unstrung,
My heart lights a flame.
A story untold, a song unsung,
My heart lights a flame.
A cultivated fire, yet a blaze withheld, a fire that must be celled, for who knows the power of an inferno unquelled?

A target locked, an arrow nocked,
I take aim.
Hands a trembling, breath a shaking,
I take aim.
A hand unfurled, an arrow loose, and for a moment, only a moment, I was engulfed.

A conflagration, a transient sensation,
My heart lights a flame.
