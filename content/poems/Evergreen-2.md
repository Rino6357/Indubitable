---
title: "Evergreen 2"
date: "2026-03-30"
category: "poem"
---

The wind carries trails of shattered sage,
Its spilled olive blood drunk by her majesty.

Roots gathered helplessly in a fragmented assuage,
Lost in the crime, scattered in the broken amnesty.

Sung were the songs of the untethered birds,
Ringing from the trees of a once familiar land.

“Retribution must be delivered for our wrongful hurts,”
A plea from the oppressed to the highest command.

Unknown claws wade through spilled blood,
Descriptions of an intruder never before seen.

A beast, a monster, risen from shadowy mud,
A nightmare from THE forgotten QUEEN.
