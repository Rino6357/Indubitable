---
title: "The firecracker"
date: "2025-05-17"
category: "poem"
---

A corridor I walk, a faint light in my stride,
The dark my companion, I found a glimmer.

The mythical end, only a myth, a hide,
In place of the sun, I find something dimmer.

With a light I lit, tried, for it had been,
An explosion of sparks, the corridor alight.

A fiery light, stabbing at the dark in its spin,
Not thunder, not flame, but you burn despite.

In my hands, the world you illuminate,
Past the corridor, past me, you radiate.

Oh, how the stars would weep, envious,
I thought myself an astrophile, oblivious,
But it was just you, your radiance, glorious.
