---
title: "THE QUEEN"
date: "2026-02-07"
category: "poem"
---

Life carried by winds unseen, 
Giving succor to a shrouded land.

Beneath the sky, Beneath the earth,
Lay what should not have been, 
A sign left by the invisible hand.

The last breath before her transient hibernation,
A message from her,
"This is the height of my acclimation."

From nothingness did it sprout,
Born of no growth nor time, 
An anomaly, just laying about.

At my doorstep, I found this treasure,

Sent by The Queen at her leisure,
Yet all I could utter,
Was "what have I done to receive the pleasure?"
