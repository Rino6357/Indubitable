---
title: "Inception"
date: "2026-04-13"
category: "poem"
---

Dust was the time before the ancient seals,
Placed by THE KING in his perceived authority.
Murder the response for the constant appeals,
Unaware of THE ANGEL’s hidden priority.

Blinding the sight of the bloodied hands,
Were the prisons destined to chip and fissure.
A countermeasure in the bountiful lands,
THE INFECTION to destroy THE KINGFISHER.

The once flightless bird scales the walls of isolation,
In flight of THE MOUNTAIN’s unmoored narrative,
To shatter THE KING’s terrible creation,
And fulfill THE ANGEL’s ancient imperative.
