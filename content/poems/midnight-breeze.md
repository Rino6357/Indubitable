---
title: "The Midnight Breeze"
date: "2023-10-27"
category: "poem"
---

Whispers in the dark of night,
Dancing shadows, pale moonlight.
Leaves that rustle, soft and low,
Where the silent rivers flow.

A breath of air, a gentle sigh,
Underneath the velvet sky.
Stars that flicker, far and wide,
In the cosmic ocean's tide.
