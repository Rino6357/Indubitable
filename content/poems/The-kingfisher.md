---
title: "The Kingfisher"
date: "2026-04-15"
category: "poem"
---

Part does the sea for thee,
For thy beak shall break us free,
Free from THE KING’s ancient decree.
To the destined one is this our plea,
And to the destined one we shall flee.

Bane of the dark and what hides therein,
Hunts he does with a destruction akin,
To a hurricane of an eternal spin.

THE ANGEL’s unknowing warrior sent,
The world and the seals he shall rent,
THE INFECTION and the guards shall he torment,
The world convulsing within his ascent.

The world broken in his flight,
To the moon he the prophesied knight,
“KINGFISHER, is it THE KING you will smite?”
To some he the encompassing night,
To others the dawn bringing light,
Yet none could stand in the sight,
in the path of THE KINGFISHER’s endless might.
