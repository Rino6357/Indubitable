---
title: "Evergreen"
date: "2026-01-11"
category: "poem"
---

The benevolent evergreen sheds her brilliant emeralds.

They dance among the whirlwind and fly through mists unseen. 

They ripple through the plains bringing forth olives and trees.

They spelunker through the caves sleeping in cracks and ravines.

They fly against the rampaging river, swimming through the luminous cascades.

Her foliage neglects none. 
Indeed, only the dead are unfamiliar to her blades.
