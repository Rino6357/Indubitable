---
title: "A dance of fire and blood"
date: "2025-05-13"
category: "poem"
---

Ripples disturb the silent surface,
The water parts, anticipating, restless.
Veins burn with fire, with purpose,
Your sword beckons, captivating, weightless.

The water shatters, cascading, a prance,
The moonlight scatters, prismatic, you advance,
Your blade flows, you surge, and we dance.

Sparks graze the pond, metal on metal,
The fire spreads, the flowers weep, a burning petal,
The vapor envelopes, an infinite battle.

A blurring motion, a fake, a promising swing,
Red breaks the surface, a splash, a clipped wing,
The sky weeps, the trees mourn, the birds sing.

Flames burn through the cacophony,
Hearts freeze with ice, with symphony.
The rain answers in desperation, a flood,
Yet we dance, charring the earth crimson with our blood.
